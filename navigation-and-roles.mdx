## User roles

| Role | Typical access |
|------|----------------|
| **Admin** | Full company settings, billing admin, users, preferences |
| **Staff** | Day-to-day operations; may exclude some admin-only pages |
| **Field tech** | Installs, tickets, limited billing/network views |
| **Client** | Portal only: own invoices, tickets, profile |

Your administrator assigns roles when creating users under **Company → Users**.

## Superadmin (platform operator)

Users marked as superadmin can:

- View the list of companies
- **View as** another company to troubleshoot without a separate login

When viewing as another company, a banner appears at the top—use **Exit view** to return to normal context.

## Billing menu (staff)

When you have billing access, the **Billing** dropdown includes:

- Dashboard, Clients, Invoices, Payments
- Failed Transactions, Write Offs
- **Reporting** — MRR, ARR, revenue by service type, taxes, churn/revenue loss, aging
- Service Templates
- HR-related entries where enabled

## Company menu

The **Company** dropdown groups operational tools:

### Network
- **Burst calculator** — MikroTik-style burst limit helper
- **Monitoring** — Host status and agents
- **RADIUS** — Users, groups, NAS, bundles, accounting
- **GIS** — Network map and infrastructure

### Other company items
- **Users** — Staff accounts
- **Preferences** — Company-wide configuration tabs
- **Leads** / **Inventory** — When enabled for your deployment

## Network Management

Reach vendor controllers via **Network Management** (hub) or **Vendor Controllers**:

- Add MikroTik, Ubiquiti UniFi, or Cambium cnMaestro controllers
- Manage devices, PPPoE, firewall, UniFi clients, Cambium SMs, etc.
- Link controllers to RADIUS NAS records

## Search

Global **Search** (where enabled) finds clients, tickets, and related records across the company.

<Info>
If a menu item is missing, your role may not include that permission, or the feature may be disabled in company preferences.
</Info>
