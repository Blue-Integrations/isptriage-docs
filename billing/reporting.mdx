---
title: Billing reporting
description: MRR, ARR, aging, taxes, and revenue reports
---

Reporting lives under **Billing → Reporting** (after Write Offs in the menu).

## Available reports

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue from active services |
| **ARR** | Annualized recurring revenue view |
| **Revenue by service type** | Breakdown by plan/category |
| **Taxes collected** | Tax amounts on invoices in range |
| **Churn / revenue loss** | Lost MRR from churned or downgraded services |
| **Aging** | AR aging buckets (current, 30, 60, 90+ days) |

## Using reports

1. Open the report from the Billing submenu.
2. Set date range or as-of date where prompted.
3. Export or print from the browser if needed.

<Tip>
Run aging and MRR reports on the same date each month for consistent month-over-month comparison.
</Tip>

## Billing dashboard vs reports

- **Billing dashboard** — snapshot KPIs and recent activity
- **Reports** — deeper cuts for finance review and board reporting

## Data accuracy

Reports depend on:

- Correct **service template** pricing and status
- Invoice and payment dates entered accurately
- Services marked inactive when clients churn

<Note>
If numbers disagree with your accounting system, verify service end dates, write-offs, and manual adjustments for the period.
</Note>
