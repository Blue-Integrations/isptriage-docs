title: Outage management
description: Planned maintenance and outage notifications
---

**Support → Outage management** sends bulk notifications to affected customers.

## When to use

- Planned maintenance windows
- Unplanned network outages
- Service degradation affecting a defined area or customer set

## Send a notification

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outage management">
    Support → Outage management.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose message">
    Enter subject and body. Use clear start/end times and impact description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose audience">
    Select clients by criteria (e.g. service area, tag, or list)—options depend on your form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Confirm and send. Email uses company mail settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Prepare a template for common outage types (fiber cut, tower maintenance, DNS issue) so NOC can send faster under pressure.
</Tip>

## Planned maintenance template

Email templates may include branded planned-maintenance layouts. Coordinate message wording with support so customer replies route to tickets if needed.

## After the outage

- Post a follow-up or close related tickets
- Update ticket subjects if customers reply to the outage email
- Document root cause in a project or internal note for postmortem

<Note>
Delivery depends on valid client email addresses and working `MAIL_*` configuration on the server.
</Note>
